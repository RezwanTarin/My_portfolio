The Capston Project

Creating Geeazy mobile application

Introduction,

In This project we will be building multi-platform mobile application for the company Geeazy. This application will allow handymen to make a profile and list their experiences, skills, contact info and preferred locations to work. It allows users to search for skilled handymen and local contractors to hire for services like renovation, electrician, plumbing, painting, window cleaning, etc. Also Customers can rate and review handymen from 1 to 5 stars based on their experience with them.

Project Vision,

The vision that we have for this multi-platform mobile application is to expand the company Geeazy to reach more people. We want more people to benefit from the companyâ€™s services. By creating mobile application, we make it easier and user friendly, so any users can access easily on their phone.

business opportunity in this product is connecting local contractors and handyman with communities and user. Business will get more customer make more money, and users find the Best handyman or contractors. Both side benefit from it and it will be good opportunity for business and users.
